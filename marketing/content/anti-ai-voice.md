# 反 AI 味写作规范

> 版本：v2.0 | 所有内容 Agent Prompt 必须引用本节

## 目标读者语感

25–35 岁美国职场人，在 Reddit/Bluesky 上说话方式：**短句、具体、略带丧、不装专家**。

## 禁止清单

### 开头（出现即判失败，需重写）

- In today's competitive job market
- Are you tired of / Are you struggling
- In this article / In this post / In this guide
- It's no secret / Let's face it
- Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional
- Did you know that
- Here's the thing / Here's why

### 词汇（能不用就不用）

leverage, utilize, delve, tapestry, landscape, game-changer, unlock, empower, robust, seamless, cutting-edge, dive in, navigate, foster, elevate, streamline, comprehensive guide

### 结构病

- 连续三个排比句
- 每段都以问句结尾
- 过度使用 em dash（—）连接从句
- 「First… Second… Third…」教科书列举（偶尔可以，不能每篇都这样）
- 结尾「In conclusion」或「To sum up」

## 必须清单

1. **具体数字或时间线**：47 applications、2 replies、3 weeks of silence
2. **一个不完美细节**：i thought、honestly、not gonna lie、week 6 of this
3. **动词为主**：cut、sent、rewrote、got ghosted（少用名词化）
4. **工具只在解法后出现**，首段只写痛

## 博客范例开头（好）

> I sent 52 applications in March. Two phone screens. Both went nowhere after round one.
>
> The recruiter at the second company told me—off the record—that my resume "read generic." Same bullets I'd used at my last job. Different companies, same document.

## 博客范例开头（差）

> In today's competitive job market, crafting a tailored resume is essential for standing out from the crowd. Whether you're applying to tech giants or startups, leveraging AI tools can empower your job search journey.

## Bluesky 范例（好）

> 47 apps last month. 2 replies. same resume every time. i finally started rewriting the bullets per company and the response rate actually moved.

## Bluesky 范例（差）

> Struggling with your job search? Unlock your potential with tailored resumes! 🚀 #JobSearch #CareerTips

## 西语补充

- 用 tú 或省略主语，不用过于正式的「usted」营销腔
- 避免「En el competitivo mercado laboral actual」
